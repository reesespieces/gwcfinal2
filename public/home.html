Find Your College

MapOurTours provides an easy, accessible way to search for the colleges you want to visit. Simply search our map by location or college name to find the college that is the perfect match for you. From there it is easy to book available campus tours. Book more than one tour and add them to your schedule to keep track of all of your college visits.

Why MapOurTours?

MapOurTours is designed to improve the process of scheduling and booking college tours by putting all of your information in one place. After signing up, a user has the ability to book any college tour.

MapOurTours saves users' information for booking a campus tour; this way the user does not have to re-enter their information every time they want to book a tour. The search map makes it easy to see what colleges are near you or near the area you are visiting. This feature gives users the ability to schedule tours with colleges that they hadn’t planned on visiting prior to using our website. MapOurTours makes the booking process a simple, accessible, and enjoyable experience so students can get the most out of their visits.

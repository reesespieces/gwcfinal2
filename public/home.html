MapOurTours provides an easy, accessible way to search for the colleges you want to visit. Simply serach our map by location or college name to find the college that is the perfect match for you. From there it is one easy step to book available campus tours. Book more than one tour and add them to your schedule to keep track of all your busy college visits.

Why MapOurTours?

MapOurTours is designed to improve the process of scheduling and booking college tours by putting all the necessary information in one place. After signing up a user has the capability of booking any college campus tour. MapOurTours saves user's information necessary for booking a campus tour; this way the user does not have to re-enter their information every time they want to book a tour. The search map keeps it easy to see what colleges are near you or near the area you are visiting. This feature will give users the ability to schedule college tours with colleges that they hadn’t planned on visiting prior to visiting our website. MapOurTours keeps the college tour booking process a simple, accessible, and enjoyable experience so students can get the most out of their visits.
